# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-07-04*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** MinionMint mints bounded AI workers from a plain-English business diagnosis.
**What it does:** MinionMint guides a non-technical user through a concierge interview and produces a Minion Blueprint: mission, memory, knowledge, approval rails, first work order, and future computer/channel needs. The product is not a generic chatbot builder because it designs the worker's operating system before feature expansion.
**Product category:** AI employee platform, computer-use agent platform, agent OS, AI operations workspace.
**Product type:** SaaS.
**Business model:** Planned subscription plus metered usage for LLM, VM minutes, communications, and storage.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** founders, solo operators, service businesses, consultants, and small teams.
**Decision-makers:** founder, owner, operator, consultant, agency lead.
**Primary use case:** convert a messy workflow into a scoped AI worker with safety rails.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Diagnose what kind of AI worker is needed.
- Define the worker's mission, knowledge, memory, and approvals.
- Prepare for future channels and computer use without overwhelming the user.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:** Minion Blueprint, approval-first rails, future managed workstation, memory/knowledge distinction, operations-board dashboard.
**How we do it differently:** Starts with job diagnosis and worker design rather than prompt editing or widget setup.
**Why customers choose us:** They want AI labor without becoming prompt engineers or agent infrastructure builders.

## Customer Language
**Words to use:** mint, Minion, blueprint, worker, mission, rails, first work order, minting bay, operations board, workstation.
**Words to avoid:** generic AI helper, chatbot builder, bot widget, starter agent.

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** calm, practical, slightly playful, operationally specific.
**Style:** plain-English, reassuring, specific about safety and next steps.
**Personality:** capable, bright, guided, trustworthy, inventive.
