ACTUAL PLATFORM THESIS

MinionMint is not a generic chatbot builder.

MinionMint is a minting desk for useful, bounded AI workers. A user describes a messy business need in plain English and receives a Minion Blueprint that defines:

- job
- brain
- memory
- knowledge sources
- operating rules
- approval boundaries
- first work order
- channel plan
- future workstation needs
- audit and usage requirements

The core promise:

Mint a scoped AI worker with a job, memory, knowledge, and safety rails before the user ever touches a prompt.

THE PROBLEM MINIONMINT SOLVES

Most people do not know how to translate a business problem into an agent system. They can describe pain such as missed leads, follow-up, inbox overload, research, client prep, repetitive admin, or support questions. They usually cannot define:

- what role the worker should have
- what it should be allowed to do
- what knowledge it needs
- what memory should persist
- what tools and channels it should get
- what actions require approval
- when computer-use is needed
- how to keep the system safe and isolated

Generic chatbot builders make users configure prompts and widgets. MinionMint should diagnose the job, propose the worker, define the safety rails, and create the first work order.

WHAT A MINION MEANS

A Minion is a small, bounded AI worker minted for a mission. It is not just a chat persona.

A Minion has:

1. Job
The outcome it is responsible for.

2. Brain
Model, role, tone, goals, rules, and escalation behavior.

3. Memory
Persistent facts, preferences, summaries, corrections, and task history.

4. Knowledge
Approved documents, websites, FAQs, SOPs, examples, videos, client context, and business rules.

5. Operating rails
What it can do alone, what needs approval, and what it must never do.

6. Channels
Chat first, then later email, SMS, voice, inboxes, and external communication surfaces.

7. Workstation
A future managed computer session with browser, apps, terminal, files, and visible screen state.

8. Ledger
Audit trail, usage, cost, approvals, last activity, and human takeover points.

HOW MINIONMINT DIFFERS FROM GENERIC CHATBOT BUILDERS

Generic chatbot builders:

- start with prompts and widget settings
- produce chat personalities
- force the user to know what to configure
- focus on knowledge-base chat
- usually stay reactive
- often have weak safety and approval models

MinionMint:

- starts with job diagnosis and worker design
- produces a Minion Blueprint
- asks adaptive questions and proposes defaults
- combines knowledge, memory, channels, approvals, and computer-use planning
- is built toward proactive AI employees
- is approval-first and tenant-isolated
- treats onboarding as a workflow audit and worker design process
